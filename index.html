Seasonal Gardening Guide

Spring Gardening: The Reawakening

As temperatures rise and daylight extends, spring presents the perfect opportunity to prepare your garden for a productive year. This season is characterized by soil preparation, early planting, and establishing the foundation for summer abundance.

Key spring tasks include:

  • Testing and amending soil based on winter conditions
  • Starting seeds indoors for warm-season crops
  • Direct sowing cold-hardy vegetables like peas, spinach, and radishes
  • Pruning fruit trees and berry bushes before bud break
  • Dividing and transplanting perennial plants

Spring is also the ideal time to establish new garden beds, install irrigation systems, and implement pest management strategies before populations explode. Pay special attention to soil temperature rather than calendar dates when determining planting times.
