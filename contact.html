Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

Our team strives to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours during business days. For urgent matters related to plant health, we offer a priority response service for members. Workshop registrations and volunteer applications typically receive confirmation within 24 hours.

Do you offer virtual gardening consultations?

Yes! We offer virtual consultations via video call for garden planning, pest identification, and troubleshooting. These 45-minute sessions are available Tuesday through Saturday and can be scheduled through our online booking system. Members receive one free virtual consultation per quarter.

Can I bring a plant sample for identification or diagnosis?

Absolutely. Our Plant Health Clinic accepts plant samples Tuesday through Friday, 10am-3pm. Please bring your sample in a sealed plastic bag with as much of the plant as possible (including roots if showing symptoms). We also accept digital photos via email for preliminary assessment.

What types of workshops do you offer for beginners?

We offer a comprehensive beginner series that includes: Soil Fundamentals, Seed Starting 101, Organic Pest Management, Seasonal Vegetable Gardening, and Container Gardening. These workshops run monthly and include both classroom instruction and hands-on garden time. Check our website for the current schedule.

Are there volunteer opportunities for experienced gardeners?

Yes, we have several specialized volunteer roles including: Garden Mentor (helping new gardeners), Plant Propagation Specialist, Compost Management, and Educational Program Assistant. We also have seasonal opportunities for harvest assistance and special event support. Contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteer@noeticcanopy.org for more information.
