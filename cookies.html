Our Cookies Policy

Learn how Noetic Canopy Conservatorium uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and how you can manage your preferences.

Understanding Our Use of Cookies

About This Policy

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your cookie preferences when visiting the Noetic Canopy Conservatorium website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve your experience. They do not contain personally identifiable information unless you've provided it to us.

How We Use Cookies

At Noetic Canopy Conservatorium, we use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled as they are necessary for core functionality.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (like language or region) to provide enhanced, personalized features.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements.

Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may also use various third-party cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how visitors engage with our website.
  • Social Media Platforms: Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest, and YouTube may set cookies when you interact with our social media content.
  • Payment Processors: If you make a donation or purchase, our payment providers may set cookies.
  • Embedded Content: Videos, maps, and other embedded content may set cookies.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have the right to choose whether to accept cookies. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on our website and limit some functionalities.

You can manage cookies through:

  1. Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. Refer to your browser's help menu for instructions.
  2. Cookie Management Tools: Various third-party tools are available to help you manage cookies.
  3. Our Cookie Controls: Use the controls below to manage your preferences for non-essential cookies.
