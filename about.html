About Noetic Canopy Conservatorium

Discover our mission, history, and the passionate team behind our commitment to blending horticultural science with mindful gardening practices.

Founded in 2008, Noetic Canopy Conservatorium emerged from a simple yet profound realization: that gardening is not merely an act of cultivation, but a holistic practice connecting mind, body, and ecosystem. Our name reflects this philosophy—Noetic relating to the mind and intellect, Canopy representing the protective and nurturing layers of a forest, and Conservatorium denoting a place of conservation and learning.

What began as a small community garden project has evolved into an internationally recognized center for horticultural excellence, ecological stewardship, and mindful gardening education. Our 15-acre conservatory grounds serve as both a living laboratory and a sanctuary for those seeking to deepen their relationship with the natural world.

At Noetic Canopy, we believe that gardens are more than collections of plants—they are carefully designed ecosystems that reflect the harmony possible when human intelligence works in concert with natural processes. Our approach combines rigorous scientific methodology with contemplative practices, creating what we call "conscious horticulture."

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To advance the art and science of gardening through innovative research, immersive education, and the promotion of ecologically responsible practices that foster deeper connections between people and the living world.

We strive to demonstrate that thoughtful cultivation can be both an act of environmental stewardship and a pathway to personal wellbeing.

Our Vision

To create a world where every garden—from balcony containers to expansive landscapes—serves as a node in a global network of sustainable, life-affirming spaces that nourish both people and planet.

We envision communities where gardening is recognized as essential infrastructure for ecological health, food security, and human flourishing.

Our History & Milestones

2008

Foundation

Noetic Canopy Conservatorium is founded by Dr. Evelyn Green with a vision to create a space where gardening is understood as both science and meditation. Initial operations begin on a 2-acre leased plot.

2012

Expansion & Recognition

Acquisition of additional land brings the conservatory to its current 15-acre size. The organization receives the National Horticultural Society's "Innovation in Gardening Education" award.

2015

Research Publications

Publication of our seminal research paper "The Mindful Garden: Cognitive Benefits of Horticultural Practice" in the Journal of Environmental Psychology establishes our reputation in the field.

2018

International Outreach

Launch of our global webinar series reaches gardeners in 47 countries. Establishment of partnership programs with botanical gardens in the UK, Japan, and Australia.

2022

Sustainable Campus

Completion of our net-zero energy visitor center featuring green roofs, rainwater harvesting, and solar-powered greenhouse operations. Recognition as a "Climate Positive" organization.

2023

Digital Transformation

Launch of comprehensive online learning platform with interactive courses, virtual garden tours, and our extensive plant database serving gardeners worldwide.
